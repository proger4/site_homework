## 1–2 likely design errors on the title page

AI decomposition: `homework/hypothesis/decomposition.md`

Based on Site.pro’s public style: direct SaaS messaging, simple product-first hierarchy, and clear CTA like **“AI Website Builder”** / **“Create New Website”**. ([Site.pro][1])

### 1. Weak brand hierarchy

**Error:** the title page likely looks too generic as a “company style draft” instead of immediately reading as **Site.pro**.

**Fix:** put the **Site.pro logo/name first**, then one clear title:

> **Company Style Draft**
> Site.pro — AI Website Builder

Avoid making decorative elements stronger than the logo/title.

### 2. Too many visual accents or wrong mood

**Error:** if the title page uses many colors, heavy gradients, or a “creative portfolio” mood, it conflicts with Site.pro’s practical product style: simple, functional, fast, no-code, easy website creation. Their public messaging focuses on “easy,” “AI,” “responsive,” “templates,” “SEO,” and “support.” ([Site.pro][1])

**Fix:** keep the cover clean:

* mostly white / light background;
* one strong accent color;
* simple rounded UI/card elements;
* short headline + subtitle;
* no overloaded illustration.

**Best short critique:**

> The title page should be more product-like and less decorative. Site.pro’s style is clear, functional, and CTA-oriented, so the cover must emphasize the logo, AI Website Builder positioning, and clean UI structure instead of complex graphics or mixed visual accents.
