## HR Process for Finding a Long-Term Salesperson in Lithuania for Site.pro

AI decomposition: `homework/hr/decomposition.md`

**Pain to close: Bridge.**
We need a person who can become a bridge between Site.pro and the Lithuanian market: understand the product, talk to potential partners, discover market gaps, give honest feedback, and turn this into a repeatable sales process.

### 1. Where to search

We should use a wide sourcing funnel, not only standard job boards.
The main sources should be LinkedIn, local Lithuanian job platforms, B2B/SaaS communities, hosting and web agency networks, startup groups, referrals, personal outreach, and people with adjacent experience in web services, hosting, SaaS, IT consulting, digital agencies, affiliate partnerships, or reseller sales.
The goal is not to find a person with one perfect background, but to collect several types of candidates: experienced B2B salespeople, people with their own client base, people from adjacent industries, and strong T-shaped candidates who understand both sales and the environment where Site.pro partners operate.

### 2. How to describe the vacancy

The vacancy should be described objectively, without over-selling the role.
We are looking for a long-term B2B Sales / Partner Manager in Lithuania who can develop partnerships with hosting companies, web agencies, SaaS businesses, resellers, and other potential partners.
The person should be able to search for partners, contact them, present Site.pro, use the product in demos, manage follow-ups, close deals, keep CRM records, and provide market feedback to the product team.
It should be clear that this is not only a “sales execution” role, but also a market development role.
The right person should understand both advantages and limitations of the product, be honest with customers, adapt the sales approach, and help the company understand what the market actually needs.

### 3. Candidate profile

The ideal candidate is T-shaped: sales is the main skill, but the person also has a broad understanding of SaaS, hosting, web studios, small business needs, marketing, digital products, and partner models.
The person should understand the difference between B2B, B2C, and B2B2C, because Site.pro is sold to partners, while the final value is often received by their end users.
The person should also understand LTV and long-term partner revenue, not only one-time sales.
Lithuanian and English are required; Russian can be useful, but should not be a hard filter.
We should not filter candidates by nationality, origin, or political views. The practical requirement is professional maturity, cross-cultural communication, neutral business behavior, and the ability to work in an international environment with different ways of thinking.

### 4. Interview funnel

The process should be organized as a funnel with several possible paths, because different strong candidates may have different strengths.

**Step 1 — Screening.**
Check language level, B2B experience, outbound experience, understanding of SaaS/IT or adjacent markets, and motivation for this specific role.

**Step 2 — Background path.**
Classify the candidate into one of several groups: experienced B2B salesperson, person with relevant client base, person from hosting/web/SaaS industry, partner/affiliate sales profile, or high-potential T-shaped candidate with strong learning ability.

**Step 3 — Product understanding.**
Ask the candidate to review Site.pro and explain: what is easy to sell, what may be difficult to sell, who the best partners are, and what objections may appear.

**Step 4 — Sales thinking test.**
Ask: “What do you think are the key metrics of our business? If you had to set your own KPI for this role, what would they be?”
A strong answer should mention qualified leads, meetings, conversion rate, active partners, partner LTV, sales cycle length, pipeline value, retention, and revenue.

**Step 5 — Practical test options.**
Use one of several tests depending on the candidate’s background:

* find 20 potential partners in Lithuania and explain why they fit;
* prepare a short pitch for a hosting company;
* prepare a demo scenario using Site.pro itself;
* describe how they would approach their existing client base;
* run a role-play with a skeptical partner;
* create a simple 30-day market entry plan.

**Step 6 — Paid trial or pilot.**
For the strongest candidates, run a short paid trial: real outreach, CRM records, first replies, objections, learning notes, and proposed next steps.
The trial should measure not only activity, but also quality of thinking, adaptability, honesty, and ability to build a repeatable process.

### 5. Competitive advantage of the role

The main advantage of the role is ownership.
The person is not hired only to follow a script, but to help Site.pro develop the Lithuanian market, build partner relationships, use the product directly in sales, and influence the company through real feedback from the market.
This fits a person who wants autonomy, long-term responsibility, international context, and a clear connection between performance and income.

### 6. Vacancy text, max 10 sentences

Site.pro is looking for a long-term B2B Sales / Partner Manager in Lithuania.
The main goal is to develop partnerships with hosting companies, web agencies, SaaS businesses, resellers, and other B2B partners.
You will search for potential partners, contact them, present Site.pro, show product demos, manage follow-ups, close deals, and keep the sales process structured in CRM.
We are looking for a person who understands both sales and the business environment where our partners operate.
Experience in B2B sales, SaaS, hosting, web services, digital agencies, affiliate sales, or reseller partnerships is a strong advantage.
You should be able to understand the product honestly: its strengths, limitations, market gaps, and real value for partners and end users.
Lithuanian and English are required; Russian is a plus, but not a key requirement.
This role requires autonomy, adaptability, cultural flexibility, and the ability to work in an international environment.
We offer a clear product, flexible work format, fixed compensation plus performance-based commission, and the opportunity to own the development of the Lithuanian market.
This role is best for a T-shaped person who wants not only to sell, but also to connect product, market, partners, and long-term revenue.
